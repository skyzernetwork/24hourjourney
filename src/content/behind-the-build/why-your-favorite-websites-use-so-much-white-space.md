# Why your favorite websites use so much white space

Modern websites are filled with empty space on purpose. While it can seem like designers are wasting room, white space helps guide the eye, improve readability, and make websites feel less overwhelming.

When every section is packed tightly together, users have a harder time figuring out where to focus. Spacing creates separation between ideas and helps important content stand out naturally.

Companies like Apple, Stripe, and Linear all use heavy spacing because it makes products feel cleaner and easier to understand. Good white space is not about making a website empty — it is about giving content room to breathe.
