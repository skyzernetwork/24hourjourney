# Why simple websites often perform better

There is a difference between a website being simple and a website feeling empty.

Simple websites remove unnecessary distractions and focus attention on the content that actually matters. Instead of filling every section with effects or animations, they prioritize readability, structure, and usability.

This often leads to:
- Faster loading times
- Better mobile experiences
- Clearer navigation
- Higher conversion rates

Minimal design is difficult because every detail becomes more noticeable. When done correctly, simplicity creates a more focused and professional experience.
