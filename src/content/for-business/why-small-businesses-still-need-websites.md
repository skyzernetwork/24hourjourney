# Why small businesses still need websites

Many small businesses rely heavily on Instagram, Facebook, or TikTok for visibility. While those platforms are useful, they should not replace a proper website.

A website gives a business complete control over its branding, content, and customer experience. It also creates a central place where customers can learn about services, pricing, locations, and contact information.

Unlike social media accounts, websites are not tied to changing algorithms or platform rules. A professional website still acts as one of the strongest trust signals a business can have online.
