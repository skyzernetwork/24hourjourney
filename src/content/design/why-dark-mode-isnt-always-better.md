# Why dark mode is not always better

Dark mode has become one of the biggest design trends over the last few years. It can make interfaces feel sleek and modern, especially for creative brands or developer-focused products.

However, dark mode is not ideal for every situation.

Bright backgrounds are often easier to read for long-form content, documentation, and blogs. They can also create stronger contrast for accessibility depending on the font and color choices being used.

The best websites choose themes based on readability and brand identity instead of simply following trends.
