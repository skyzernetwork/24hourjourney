## Overview

Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information 24 Hour Journey collects when you visit the site, how that information is used, and what rights you have regarding your data.

24 Hour Journey is a blog. It does not sell products, require account creation, or process payments. As a result, the amount of personal information collected is minimal by design.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Voluntarily

If the site includes a contact form, comment section, or newsletter sign-up, any information you submit — such as your name or email address — is provided voluntarily by you. We only use that information to respond to your enquiry or deliver the service you signed up for. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, 24 Hour Journey may collect certain non-personally identifiable information automatically when you visit. This can include:

- Your browser type and version
- The pages you visit and time spent on each
- The referring URL (where you came from)
- Your approximate geographic region (country or city level, not precise location)
- Device type (desktop, tablet, mobile)

This data is collected in aggregate and is used solely to understand how readers interact with the site and to improve content and performance.

---

## Cookies

24 Hour Journey may use cookies — small text files stored in your browser — to improve site functionality and analyse traffic patterns. These are typically limited to:

- **Analytics cookies** — used to understand visitor behaviour in aggregate (e.g. page views, session duration)
- **Preference cookies** — used to remember optional settings like theme preferences, if applicable

We do not use cookies to track you across other websites or build advertising profiles. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, though some site functionality may be affected.

If third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or Plausible) are in use, they operate under their own respective privacy policies.

---

## Third-Party Services

Some content or functionality on this site may be powered by third-party services. These providers may collect data independently under their own privacy policies. Examples include:

- **Analytics providers** — to measure site traffic and usage
- **Font or asset CDNs** — to serve web fonts and static resources
- **Embedded content** — such as videos or external media, which may set their own cookies

We aim to minimise third-party data collection and prefer privacy-respecting tools where possible.

## How We Use Your Information

Any information collected by 24 Hour Journey is used only to:

- Operate and improve the site
- Respond to enquiries or requests you submit
- Understand readership trends and improve article quality
- Send emails you have explicitly opted into (such as newsletters)

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond running the site itself.

---

## Data Retention

If you contact us or subscribe to any communications, your information is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil that purpose. Analytics data is retained in aggregate form and is not linked to personally identifiable information.

You may request deletion of any personal information you have provided to us at any time by getting in touch through the site's contact channels.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

- The right to access the personal data we hold about you
- The right to request correction of inaccurate data
- The right to request deletion of your data
- The right to withdraw consent for communications you opted into
- The right to lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us directly.

## Children's Privacy

24 Hour Journey is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through this site, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it promptly.

Articles on this site may link to external websites. Once you leave 24 Hour Journey, this Privacy Policy no longer applies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of linked sites and encourage you to review their policies independently.
