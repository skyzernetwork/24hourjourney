# Local landscaping company redesign

The original website had outdated branding, slow loading times, and no clear way for visitors to request a quote. Most users were leaving the homepage within seconds.

The redesign focused on three things:
- Simplifying the homepage
- Improving mobile responsiveness
- Making quote forms easier to access

After launch, the business saw a noticeable increase in phone calls and quote requests. Most of the traffic came from mobile users, which showed how important responsive layouts had become for local service companies.
