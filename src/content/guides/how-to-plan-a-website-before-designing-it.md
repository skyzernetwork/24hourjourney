# How to plan a website before designing it

A lot of people jump directly into colors, logos, and layouts before figuring out what the website actually needs to accomplish.

Before designing anything, it helps to answer a few questions:
- Who is the audience?
- What actions should visitors take?
- What pages are actually necessary?
- What information matters most?

Planning the structure first makes the design process much easier. It also prevents websites from becoming cluttered or confusing later in development.
