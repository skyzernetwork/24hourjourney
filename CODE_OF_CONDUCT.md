# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Skyzer Networks is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and professional environment for everyone.

We expect all interactions related to this project to be respectful and constructive.

---

## 📌 Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:
- Be respectful in communication
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, or offensive behavior
- Respect differing opinions and feedback
- Communicate constructively

---

## ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

The following will not be tolerated:
- Harassment or abusive language
- Hate speech of any kind
- Trolling or disruptive behavior
- Attempting to exploit or damage the project or website

---

## ⚠️ Enforcement

Violations may result in:
- Removal of access to communication channels
- Blocking or banning from project interaction
- Reporting to appropriate authorities if necessary

---

## 📬 Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to:

📧 skyzernetworks@gmail.com

All reports will be reviewed confidentially.
